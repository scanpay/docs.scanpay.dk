Payment Window

The payment window is where your customers enter their payment details. The payment window is accessed through payment links. You can either redirect the customer to the payment window, or you can embed the window in your website or app.

The payment window is extremely lightweight and probably the fastest in the world, but more importantly, it is auditable, secure and stable. For security and privacy reasons, we do not use any third-party frameworks or services.
