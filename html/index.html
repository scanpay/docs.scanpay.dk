Getting started

Scanpay is a software company from Denmark. We have developed a payment platform that allows companies to accept payments online. The platform is programmed in C and designed with a security by design approach. The emphasis has been on developing a secure, stable and efficient platform with a small and auditable codebase.

We have put much thought into our API and hope you will enjoy working with it. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to email us or chat with us on our IRC server:

  • Server: irc.scanpay.dev:6697 #scanpay
  • Webchat: irc.scanpay.dev

We have a dashboard where merchants can manage their account settings and payment transactions. To access the dashboard, you must create a personal user account, which you can use to create and manage multiple shops.

Partners, or merchants with more than 20 shops, can request access to our partner portal, where they can easily manage multiple shops.

Shops can have multiple users with individual privileges, and we encourage our users to enforce the principles of least privilege (PoLP).
