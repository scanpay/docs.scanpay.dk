Getting started

Scanpay is a software company from Denmark. We have developed a payment platform that allows companies to accept payments online. The platform is programmed from scratch in C and designed with a security by design approach, making security considerations the core of our engineering process. The emphasis has been on developing a secure, stable and efficient platform with a small and auditable codebase.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to e-mail us at support@scanpay.dk. You can always chat with us on IRC at libera.chat #scanpay (webchat).
