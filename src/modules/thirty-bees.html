Thirty Bees

We have developed an official payment module for Thirty Bees 1.0 and higher. The module also works with PrestaShop 1.6. The module is developed and maintained by scanpay. Installation

  1. Download the Thirty Bees Scanpay extension here.
  2. Enter the Thirty Bees admin and navigate to Modules and Services.
  3. Click the 'Add a new module' button in the upper right corner. Select the zip file you just downloaded and press 'Upload this module'.
  4. Scroll down to find the Scanpay module and press the green 'install' button

Configuration

  1. Enter the admin, navigate to Modules and Services and press the Payment and Gateways category.
  2. Find 'Scanpay' and press 'Configure'.
  3. Locate the API-key field and copy an API-key from the dashboard into the field.
  4. Copy the content of the Ping URL field into the corresponding field in the Scanpay dashboard and save it. After saving press the 'Test Ping' button.
  5. Return to the Thirty Bees admin and press the 'save' button in the lower right corner.
  6. Verify that the previously yellow box below Ping URL has turned green and says 'Ok!'.
  7. Navigate away to Modules and Services > Payment in the left sidebar.
